About Us

Thank you for visiting Brewery Dog!

Brewery Dog is a webpage that helps you to find the breweries in your city or in any cities that you prefer to visit in the future. The application is made to make life easier for anyone who loves beer and traveling. The webpage is very easy to use as you just have to enter the name of the city in the search box and upon clicking submit button, the page will display the list of the breweries in the city. Upon clicking on one of the brewery name, the page will open up a google map, that leads you to the direction and address of the brewery.

We four founders and friends, Sonika Poudyal, J.Connor O'Brien, Alan Aynbund and Rohan Nair, take great pride in our brewery locater application and have spent quite a time creating it. We hope you will use our application while staying home or travelling somewhere for a chill evening with loved ones.
